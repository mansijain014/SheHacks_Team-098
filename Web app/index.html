Menstruation- A Taboo No More.

This discussion should be open to all genders, because everyone should be educated on menstruation, not just people who experience it. This discussion is vital in order to erase misconceptions about menstruation and could help further education throughout the world.

About Us

We want women to be vocal about Menstruation, We want Men to understand and support the viable perspective about Menstruation. Many men and women never got a real education about menstruation. They may have been told what it biologically was in biology class in schools, but a lot of real questions go unanswered, and unasked. We believe PERIODS are for EVERYONE as much as it is important for everyone to be socially aware about it.
